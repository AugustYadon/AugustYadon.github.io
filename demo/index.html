Plunker is a game created Solely (art, code, ideas, etc) by August Yadon at his company Auggo Doggo Games. The game is made for the Vive and is a virtual reality title. It is targetted to go on the Steam market in January. It's very hard to make and test anything without having a few blind spots. Having other people test helps by revealing game-breaking movements or actions that have not been tried during development. It's also an invaluable way to get data about parts of the game that may be out of pace, or otherwise not as obvious as previously thought. All feedback is helpful and welcome. If we do get enough testers to accomplish our goals, then we may take a rain check on your testing slot and ask if you can come back for a test in the future.

Here's a brief informal introduction to Plunker: You wander around some very goofy, stressful, funny VR spaces trying to find things and play minigames to accomplish your objectives. It's VR so it feels very immersive which has an impact on how funny, stressful, shocking everything feels. You shouldn't know exactly where to go, that's sort of the point, it's a funny game where you wander around small spaces and poke stuff. In January it will be on sale via Steam for somewhere between $10 and $30.
