Game Development & Streaming

Auggo Doggo Games is focused on making fun games with interesting and often experimental new mechanics. Our current project is 'Plunker', a unique VR game with a lot of amazingly fun mechanics and hilarious 'look like an idiot while your friends secretly record you from outside the game' moments.

To read more, watch a trailer, and download the free demo, click here.

If you're here looking to contact the company, emails can be sent to AuggoDoggoGames@gmail.com.



As often as I can (Usually 5 days a week, 4 to 9 hours a day), I will stream every part of the development of my current project. Along with seeing the many successes and [temporary]failures of game development, the audience usually gets to chip in on the project. I appreciate everyone who stops in with encouragement and input (they are far more valuable than they could possibly know).
