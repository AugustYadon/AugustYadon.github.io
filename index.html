About Us

AuggoDoggo Games is focused on making fun games with interesting and often experimental new mechanics. The games are developed in C# in the Unity3D Engine, and the VR titles make use of as much of Valve's "SteamVR Unity plugin" as is helpful. If you're here looking to contact August, emails can be sent to AuggoDoggoGames@gmail.com, or you can check out the "Contact" section of the site to send a message.

If you're here to see and play games and demos, hold on, because they'll be up soon enough.
